⚡ Piezoelectric Energy Generation System

Smart Energy for Future Electric Vehicles

📌 Introduction

Piezoelectric energy is a modern method of generating electricity using pressure. When mechanical stress is applied to piezoelectric materials, electrical energy is produced. This technology can be used in roads, footsteps, and platforms to generate power.

Our project focuses on converting human movement or vehicle pressure into electrical energy and using it efficiently for real-world applications such as EV charging.

⚙️ Working Principle

The system works based on the piezoelectric effect. When pressure is applied on the plates:

  • Mechanical energy is converted into electrical energy
  • The generated voltage is small but continuous
  • Multiple plates are connected to increase output

This energy is then passed through circuits for storage and further usage.

🔋 Energy Storage & Amplification

The electricity generated from piezoelectric plates is initially low in voltage and cannot be used directly. So the following steps are performed:

  • 🔹 The energy is collected and stored in batteries or capacitors
  • 🔹 A rectifier circuit converts AC to DC
  • 🔹 A voltage regulator stabilizes the power
  • 🔹 A step-up transformer increases the voltage

After amplification, the energy is sent to a power station where it is stored and distributed.

This stored energy can then be used to charge:

  • 🚗 Electric Cars
  • 🏍️ Electric Bikes
  • 🔋 Other battery-based devices

Thus, small energy generated from footsteps can be converted into useful power for large-scale applications.

🚀 Applications

  • Smart Roads
  • Railway Stations
  • Shopping Malls
  • EV Charging Stations
  • Public Walkways
