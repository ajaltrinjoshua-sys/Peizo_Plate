⚡ Smart Piezoelectric Energy

Power from Every Step

🔹 Introduction

This project generates electricity from footsteps using piezoelectric plates. When pressure is applied, mechanical energy is converted into electrical energy. This system can be used in smart cities for sustainable energy production.

⚙️ Working Principle

When a person walks over the plate, pressure is applied which generates voltage. Multiple plates together increase power output and improve efficiency.

The energy produced is collected and stored in batteries for later use.

🏭 Energy Storage & Power Station

The generated energy is stored in batteries. This energy is then sent to a power station where it is amplified using converters to increase voltage levels.

After amplification, it becomes usable for real applications like EV charging.

🚗 EV Charging Applications

The amplified energy is used in charging stations for electric bikes and cars. This promotes green energy and reduces pollution.
