⚡ Piezoelectric Plates Project

Smart Energy Generation using Footsteps

Introduction

Piezoelectric plates are smart materials that generate electricity when pressure is applied. This project demonstrates how mechanical energy from human footsteps can be converted into electrical energy.

This concept is highly useful in crowded areas such as railway stations, shopping malls, and highways, where continuous movement of people can produce a significant amount of energy.

By implementing this technology, we can create a sustainable and eco-friendly energy source that reduces dependence on traditional power generation methods.

Working Principle

The working principle of piezoelectric plates is based on the piezoelectric effect. When pressure or force is applied to certain materials, they produce an electric charge.

In this project, when a person steps on the plate, the mechanical stress is converted into electrical energy. This generated energy is collected and stored for later use.

Multiple plates can be connected together to increase the amount of energy generated. This makes the system scalable and efficient for real-world applications.

Energy Storage & Power Station

The electricity generated from the piezoelectric plates is initially small and needs to be stored efficiently. This is done using rechargeable batteries or capacitors.

The stored energy is then sent to a power station where it is regulated and amplified using transformers and converters. This ensures that the voltage is increased to a usable level.

This step is very important because raw energy generated from footsteps is not enough for direct usage. Hence, proper storage and voltage amplification are required.

EV Charging Applications

After amplification, the energy can be used for charging electric vehicles such as bikes and cars. This provides a clean and renewable energy source for EV charging stations.

This technology can be implemented in parking areas, highways, and public spaces, where vehicles can be charged using energy generated from human movement.

This reduces the load on conventional electricity sources and promotes green energy usage.

⚡ Energy Flow Demonstration

The energy generated from footsteps follows a systematic flow process before being used for real applications. Initially, the pressure applied on the piezoelectric plates generates electrical signals.

These signals are collected and stored in batteries where the energy is accumulated over time. Later, the stored energy is processed and amplified to a higher voltage level.

Finally, the amplified energy is used for charging electric vehicles, making the system efficient and practical.
